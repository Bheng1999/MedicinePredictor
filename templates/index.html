About The Project

Why MediPredict?

Revolutionizing pharmacy inventory management with cutting-edge predictive analytics

report_problem

The Problem

Most pharmacies lack intelligent systems to classify high-demand medicines, leading to costly operational issues including stockouts, overstocking, and expired inventory. Existing systems don't leverage advanced ML models to categorize medicines into clear demand levels.

lightbulb

Our Solution

MediPredict implements a secure, easy-to-use prediction platform using XGBoost classification to analyze historical sales data. Our system categorizes medicines into high, medium, or low demand levels, enabling pharmacies to optimize inventory and improve patient care.

local_pharmacy About MediPredict

MediPredict is an intelligent medicine demand prediction system designed to help Philippine pharmacies optimize their inventory management through advanced machine learning.

psychology Technology

Built with Django web framework and XGBoost machine learning algorithm. The system classifies medicine demand into Low, Medium, and High categories based on historical sales data and quantile-based thresholds.

school Academic Project

This is a capstone project developed by BS Information Technology students at Eastern Visayas State University, Tacloban City for Academic Year 2024-2025.

waving_hand Welcome to MediPredict

Follow these simple steps to start using MediPredict for your pharmacy's demand predictions.

person_add Step 1: Create an Account

Click the "Register" button and fill in your details including name, email, and a secure password. Your account will be created with pharmacist privileges.

login Step 2: Login to Your Account

After registration, use your username and password to log in. You'll be directed to your personalized dashboard.

cloud_upload Step 3: Upload Your Data

Navigate to the Upload section and submit your pharmacy's historical sales data in CSV format. The system will process and validate your data.

insights Step 4: View Predictions

Once your data is processed, view demand predictions on the dashboard. Medicines are categorized as High, Medium, or Low demand.

dashboard Using the Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of all predictions and key statistics.

  • Filter by Period: Select a date range to view predictions for specific time periods.
  • Filter by Category/Brand: Narrow down results by medicine category or brand name.
  • View Charts: Interactive charts show demand distribution and trends.
  • Reset Filters: Click the reset button to clear all applied filters.
cloud_upload Uploading Data

To generate predictions, you need to upload historical sales data.

  • Prepare CSV File: Ensure your file has columns for medicine name, category, brand, quantity sold, and date.
  • Upload File: Go to Upload section and select your CSV file.
  • Validate Data: The system will check for errors and duplicates.
  • Train Model: After successful upload, train the prediction model.
search Searching Medicines

Find specific medicines quickly using the search feature.

  • Quick Search: Type medicine name in the search bar.
  • Advanced Filters: Use category and brand filters for refined results.
  • View Details: Click on any medicine to see detailed prediction data.
quiz Frequently Asked Questions
What is MediPredict? expand_more

MediPredict is a web-based medicine demand prediction system that uses XGBoost machine learning to analyze historical pharmacy sales data and classify medicines into high, medium, or low demand categories. This helps pharmacies optimize inventory and reduce waste.

What file format is required for data upload? expand_more

MediPredict accepts CSV (Comma-Separated Values) files. Your file should include columns for medicine name, category, brand, quantity sold, and transaction date. A template is available in the upload section.

How accurate are the predictions? expand_more

The prediction accuracy depends on the quality and quantity of historical data provided. Our XGBoost model typically achieves high accuracy rates when trained with sufficient data spanning multiple months or years.

What do High, Medium, and Low demand mean? expand_more

High Demand: Medicines that are predicted to sell quickly and should be stocked in larger quantities.
Medium Demand: Medicines with moderate sales velocity requiring regular restocking.
Low Demand: Slower-moving items that can be stocked in smaller quantities to avoid expiration.

Is my pharmacy data secure? expand_more

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted during transmission and storage. Each user can only access their own pharmacy's data through role-based authentication.

How often should I retrain the model? expand_more

We recommend retraining the model whenever you upload significant new data (e.g., monthly). This ensures predictions reflect the latest sales trends and seasonal patterns.
